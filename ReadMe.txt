Current project was proposed for comparative analysis of three pricing methods:
Stoetzel`s methods,
Lipovetsky`s method,
van Westendorp`s pricesensitivity meter (PSM).

The attached dataset is the data collected from social survey with four question about
prices that are considered by an interviewed person as too low, low, high and too high
for purchasing a private tutor`s services.
